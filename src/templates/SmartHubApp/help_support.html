Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

Why can't I access my account?

Your account may be pending verification or temporarily locked. Contact support if the issue continues.

My fee payment is not reflected.

Payment updates may take a short time to process. If not updated after 24 hours, submit a support ticket.

How do I register courses?

Login to your portal, open Course Registration, select available units and submit.
